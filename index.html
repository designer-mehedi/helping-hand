Our Donation Plan

We are making sure to use the donations in a right way and give help to those who really need

Food for a family

We are ensuring food for the affected families. We are raising funds and collecting foods and give the foods to the poor helpless people.

Shelter for a family

People whose home is affected by the flood and who lose their home we are giving them a shelter so that they can overcome this tricky period.

Education for a child

In this flood many children are away from education and their institutions are also needed to be rebuilt. We are giving them a chance to start again.
